## Learning Points

### BTST Strategy Primer

BTST (Buy Today Sell Tomorrow) captures overnight momentum:

1. **Entry**: Buy in the last 90 minutes when the stock closes near its high with above-average volume
2. **Edge**: Institutional accumulation that pushed price to close near the high often carries into the next session
3. **Risk**: Overnight gap-down (the single biggest risk) — global events, sector news, or broad market selloffs can gap the stock below your stop at open
4. **Exit**: Next day by 10:30 AM or at target. Trail stop after +1.5%. Max hold: 2 days.

### Key Filters Explained

| Filter | Why It Matters |
|--------|----------------|
| Close position ≥ 0.80 | Stock closed in top 20% of range = strong demand into close |
| Above VWAP | Institutional buyers were net-positive today = bullish bias |
| Nifty not bearish | Broad market trend must support overnight longs |
| Volume surge > 1.3× | Higher than normal volume confirms institutional interest |
| Not overextended | Stock hasn't already moved too far — room for overnight continuation |
| RR ≥ 1.2 | Minimum risk-reward ratio to ensure edge survives transaction costs |
