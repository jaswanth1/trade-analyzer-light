## 1. The Framework: Wyckoff Market Cycle

Every major correction follows four phases:

```
[PEAK] → [MARKDOWN] → [CAPITULATION] → [ACCUMULATION] → [MARKUP] → [NEW PEAK]
```

| Phase | What Happens | How to Detect | How to Trade |
|-------|-------------|---------------|-------------|
| **Markdown** | Smart money distributes, price declines, breadth deteriorates | Lower highs, lower lows, FII selling, rising VIX | Reduce exposure, sell rallies |
| **Capitulation** | Fear peaks, panic selling, "get me out at any price" | VIX spike (often >30), volume surge, 3%+ single-day drops, extreme oversold RSI | DO NOT SELL. This is the worst time to exit. |
| **Accumulation** | Smart money buys quietly, price bases, VIX normalizes | Breadth improves (>40% above EMA20), volume decreases, higher lows form | Start building positions on dips |
| **Markup** | Price breaks above resistance, trend reversal confirmed | Nifty reclaims 20-EMA, breadth >60%, VIX <16 | Full position sizing, ride the trend |

### 4.2 The Two Types of Bottoms

**Type A: "V-Bottom" (Sharp reversal)**
- VIX spikes dramatically (>30 or at least 3×)
- Single massive down day (-3%+) followed by equally massive reversal
- Volume surges to 2×+ normal
- 5-day recovery: +5 to +9%
- Examples: 2019 NBFC (+8.1% in 5 days), 2020 COVID (+8.8%)
- **This is the classic "capitulation" — you feel it in real time**

**Type B: "Rounded Bottom" (Gradual base formation)**
- VIX stays elevated (18-23) but doesn't spike dramatically
- Multiple tests of support over 2-4 weeks
- Volume is normal — no panic, no surge
- 5-day recovery: +1.9 to +2.7%
- Examples: 2022 Russia, 2023 Adani, 2024-25 FII
- **This is the "accumulation" pattern — harder to identify in real time**

> **Critical learning:** Not all bottoms have a capitulation "event." The more dangerous pattern is the Type B rounded bottom where the market simply stops going down, consolidates for weeks, then quietly starts trending up. Most traders miss this because they're waiting for a VIX>30 panic that never comes.

## 8. How Will You Know Each Phase Has Started?

### Capitulation Confirmed When:
- [ ] VIX spikes >25 in a single day (or single week)
- [ ] Nifty drops >3% in one session
- [ ] Volume hits 2×+ normal on the down day
- [ ] Financial media headlines shift to "market crash" / "panic" language
- [ ] OR: 3+ weeks of sideways with VIX declining (Type B quiet bottom)

### Accumulation Confirmed When:
- [ ] VIX consistently below 20 for 5+ days
- [ ] Breadth climbs from 23% to 35%+
- [ ] Nifty forms 2+ higher lows over 2+ weeks
- [ ] FII selling decelerates (<₹1,000Cr/day avg)
- [ ] Sector rotation: cyclicals start outperforming defensives

### Markup Confirmed When:
- [ ] Nifty reclaims 20-EMA (currently 24,541) on a CLOSING basis
- [ ] Breadth rises above 50%
- [ ] VIX drops below 16
- [ ] FII turns net buyer
- [ ] ORB and pullback strategies start working consistently again (check via backtest)
